What Is Groptimizer ?

Groptimizer is a community-driven organization dedicated to optimizing grocery resources. Our mission is to redistribute nearly expired food by delivering it to food banks, homeless shelters, and individuals facing food insecurity.
how it works

About Us

Welcome to Groptimizer, an initiative led by passionate students from PSG College of Technology committed to addressing food waste and hunger. We are dedicated to rescuing surplus, nearly expired food and redirecting it to food banks, homeless shelters, and individuals in need. Through strategic partnerships with local businesses, we aim to minimize food waste while providing sustenance to those facing food insecurity. Join us in creating a more sustainable, compassionate community where no one goes hungry. Together, we can make a meaningful difference.
Recycle - reuse - reduce

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and alleviate hunger by collecting and redistributing nearly expired food to food banks, homeless shelters, and individuals in need, fostering a more sustainable and compassionate community.
